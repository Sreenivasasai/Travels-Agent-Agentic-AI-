## 🗓️ Day-wise Itinerary
Based on the provided preferences, here's a detailed day-wise itinerary for the 3-day trip to Ooty:

Day 1:
- Morning: Visit the Ooty Lake and take a boat ride. Explore the surrounding areas and enjoy the scenic views.
- Afternoon: Head to the Doddabetta Peak, the highest point in Ooty, and enjoy panoramic views of the town.
- Evening: Visit the Ooty Botanical Gardens and explore the variety of flora on display.

Day 2:
- Morning: Visit the Pykara Lake and take a boat ride. Enjoy the scenic views and explore the surrounding areas.
- Afternoon: Head to the Tea Museum and learn about the history and production of tea in Ooty.
- Evening: Explore the local market and try some street food.

Day 3:
- Morning: Visit the Emerald Lake and take a boat ride. Enjoy the scenic views and explore the surrounding areas.
- Afternoon: Head to the Ooty Rose Garden and explore the variety of roses on display.
- Evening: Visit the Thread Garden and explore the unique art on display.
